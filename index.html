CSS3Butt.in

Capitalising on Font Awesome and HSLa, we quickly, and easily create gorgeous CSS3 buttons. With Sliders, (Butt)in-line text editing, and auto vendor-prefixing, you barely have to do any work. Just make sure you include the font-awesome folder in your project, link to it appropriately, and you're good to go!

    Instructions

  • 1. Grab Font Awesome from the Butt.in(link) above
  • 2. Follow the installation instructions at Font Awesome (drop it in your project, link to its css)
  • 3. Create your butt.in!
